Our Story

HYGIENAX is more than just a cleaning products brand – it’s a commitment to making hygiene simple, affordable, and effective for everyone. What started as a personal mission to create high-quality cleaning solutions for my own space has now grown into a brand dedicated to helping homes, hotels, restaurants, and offices maintain a cleaner and safer environment.

I’m Devesh, an engineering student with a passion for solving real problems. Through research, experimentation, and feedback, I’ve developed products that are reliable, safe, and long-lasting. Every product is carefully crafted to deliver the best results while being easy to use and accessible.

At HYGIENAX, we believe that good hygiene is essential for better health, productivity, and peace of mind. Our goal is to provide solutions that not only clean but also protect and enhance everyday living.

Join us on this journey towards smarter cleaning and a healthier lifestyle – because hygiene shouldn’t be complicated, it should be HYGIENAX.
